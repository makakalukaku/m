FAQ

What is CYBRO?
CYBRO is a secure AI-based yield aggregator platform on the Blast blockchain for non-professional crypto investors. CYBRO offers multiple investment vaults and strategies tailored to the user's needs.
How do I buy a $CYBRO token?
You can buy $CYBRO with cryptocurrency or by using a credit card. To purchase $CYBRO, you need to connect a wallet that supports the Blast blockchain, and then select your preferred payment method. Please visit this page for more information: https://docs.cybro.io/cybro/usdcybro-token/how-to-buy
Is investing in CYBRO secure?
Yes, CYBRO's team and code have been verified by external auditors. The CYBRO team has successfully completed two KYC audits conducted by CertiK and Assure DeFi. Additionally, CertiK has audited CYBRO's smart contracts. To ensure security and reliability, CYBRO offers a bug bounty program with rewards of up to $25,000 for identifying vulnerabilities in their smart contracts and dApp.
When is the TGE?
The Token Generation Event (TGE) is scheduled for Q3 2024, though the date may be adjusted based on market conditions.
What is CYBRO listing price?
The listing price is $0.06 per CYBRO
What is the token contract address?
The contract addresses could be found in the documentation: https://docs.cybro.io/cybro/usdcybro-token/smart-contracts-and-addresses
Where can I find my tokens?
After purchasing tokens, you will be able to check your balance by connecting your wallet on the website:  https://cybro.io/ . The tokens will appear in your wallet after the Token Generation Event (TGE) which is scheduled for Q3 2024.
When will the token be listed on exchanges?
The token will be listed on both centralized exchanges (CEXes) and decentralized exchanges (DEXes) following the Token Generation Event (TGE). The exact list of exchanges will be announced as the TGE date approaches.
When will CYBRO launch?
The development of the CYBRO earning marketplace is currently underway, with the initial release scheduled for Q2 of 2024. Stay updated by following our Twitter, Discord or Telegram to stay tuned, and sign up for the waitlist on this page to be among the first to use CYBRO.
